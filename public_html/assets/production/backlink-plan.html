The Core Insight — Two Parallel Strategies

Cannabis community links (forums, Reddit, YouTube, cannabis media) are high-relevance but require real participation and face platform restrictions from high-DA mainstream sites.

Clean hydroponics links (VPD chart sites, Atlas Scientific, hydroponic retailers, sensor companies) have no cannabis stigma, are often higher domain authority, and require only one thing: a genuinely useful VPD chart or DWC guide. The site's chart assets are already built. This path exists right now and is being underused.

Run both tracks simultaneously. The hydroponics track builds domain authority fast. The cannabis community track builds the audience that finds and shares everything else.

Realistic Expectation Setting

New subdomain, no existing authority: expect 60–90 days before any indexed backlinks move the crawl needle in Google Search Console. The "Discovered — currently not indexed" URLs should begin crawling within 30 days of consistent crawl budget improvements (robots.txt fix already done) and 2–3 real external links pointing to priority pages.

Target after 4 months: 8–12 real, indexed backlinks from named domains. That is enough to move from "Discovered" to "Crawled" status on the thin pages, and to begin ranking for long-tail queries the site is already optimized for.

The compounding asset: The YouTube series. When Episode 01 publishes as a cinematic piece with a well-produced companion page, the video description alone creates multiple indexed external signals pointing to the site. That single event may do more for crawl budget than months of manual outreach.
