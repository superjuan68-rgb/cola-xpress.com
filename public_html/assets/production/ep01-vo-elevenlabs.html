Post-Generation Notes

Lines to Regenerate if They Sound Wrong "I slept." — must sound like quiet relief, not tiredness. "Yes." — honest and direct, not dramatic or emphatic. "It's optional." — should feel like the final word in a long conversation, not a tagline. "The craft got quieter, not gone." — callback line, steady pace, no uplift at the end.
Paragraph Spacing in Projects In ElevenLabs Projects, a blank line between paragraphs adds a natural short pause on top of any break tags. If specific gaps feel too long, shorten the break time value. If the voice rushes between lines with no break tag, add a blank line between them.
Editing the VO into the Timeline Import all five act WAVs onto a single VO track in sequence. The SSML silences are baked in — do not add additional gaps at act joins. The raw silence after "I slept." (3.0s) and after "It's optional." (4.0s) are load-bearing — do not cut them short in the edit.
Sound Design Layering The VO silence blocks are where enclosure hum, ambient sound, and music breathe. Mix those layers so they're audible under the silence but don't compete with speech. Reference: the VO should sit 6–8 dB above the music during lines; the music and hum should be 3–4 dB below VO level during silences.
