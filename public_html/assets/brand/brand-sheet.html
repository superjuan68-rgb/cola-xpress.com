Voice and Tone
Write this way
  • Calm, precise, declarative
  • Sensory — smell, texture, aroma, feel
  • Educational but never condescending
  • Restrained — let the concept land, then move on
  • Process-obsessed, not result-obsessed
  • "Quality is not created at harvest. It is preserved after it."
  • "A rushed dry destroys volatile compounds."
  • "The reservoir speaks before the canopy does."
Never write this way
  • Fire, gas, bro, smacks, insane yield
  • "Yo what's up guys welcome back"
  • Hype language around THC numbers
  • Exaggerated reactions or meme tone
  • "Smash the like button" or "limited spots"
  • Stoner clichés or cultural coding
  • Max yield bragging as success metric
  • Marketing speak without substance
Thumbnail Anatomy
Must include
  • 1
    Dark background — #060608 or near black
  • 2
    LED cabinet glow (teal/blue radial) — visual anchor
  • 3
    Episode badge — small, red, top placement
  • 4
    Headline — Cormorant Garamond, 72–90px, max 6 words
  • 5
    Accent word in red — "craft" / "cannabis" / "automation"
  • 6
    ColaXpress lockup — bottom left, small, not dominant
  • 7
    Optional: environmental data bottom right (temp/RH/VPD)
Avoid
  • ✕
    Shocked or exaggerated face
  • ✕
    Cluttered background or busy room
  • ✕
    Giant arrows or circle callouts
  • ✕
    Neon or oversaturated color
  • ✕
    Yield weight as headline hook
  • ✕
    Text covering more than 40% of frame
  • ✕
    Stock photo feel — must look cinematic
