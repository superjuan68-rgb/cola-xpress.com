The ColaXpress Grow Series

Can automation produce craft cannabis?

This series documents a complete craft cannabis grow using the VIVOSUN vGrow cabinet, DWC reservoir, and humidifier — from first seed to cured jar. Every episode covers what the environment does, what the plant reveals, and what the finish requires.

Most grow content chases yield. This series chases quality. The cabinet, the roots, the humidity, and the post-harvest discipline are the main characters. The question the whole run is trying to answer: can a controlled automated environment actually produce craft flower?

The system VIVOSUN vGrow cabinet. DWC reservoir. Humidifier. Sensors. One contained microclimate.
The question Can automation maintain the environmental discipline that craft cannabis actually requires?
The measure Not weight. Quality — terpene expression, harvest timing, drying discipline, jar behavior.
Series premise

The hero of this series is not the grower. It is the system.

The vGrow cabinet maintains the climate. The DWC reservoir feeds and oxygenates the root zone. The humidifier mediates vapor pressure. The sensors read truth the eye cannot. Every episode shows what happens when those systems hold — and what the plant asks for when they drift.

This is not a tutorial channel. It is a craft cultivation documentary. The grow will have tension points. The environment will ask for adjustments. The finish will demand patience. That is the whole point: showing what it actually takes to bring craft discipline into a small automated room.

What makes this different

Most channels measure success at harvest. We measure it when the jar is opened six weeks later.

  • No yield bragging. Quality is the only metric.
  • Drying and curing get full episodes, not footnotes.
  • The humidifier, reservoir, and sensors are characters, not background gear.
  • Every decision is explained in terms of what it protects, not what it proves.
