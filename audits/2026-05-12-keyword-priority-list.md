## What to do with this list

1. **Track positions weekly.** Use any of: Ahrefs, Semrush, Mangools, or free Google Search Console (Performance → filter by query). Note baseline this week.
2. **For each Tier 1 keyword:**
   - Search the query in incognito.
   - Note the top 5 ranking URLs.
   - Compare YOUR target page's word count, image count, schema markup against the top 5.
   - Identify the ONE thing you don't have that they do (usually: more FAQ schema, more original images, more recent date, more inbound links).
   - Fix that thing.
3. **Build internal-link routes to Tier 1 pages.** Every page in the same cluster should link to its Tier 1 anchor with descriptive anchor text. The 5 section index pages should each link to their cluster's Tier 1 commercial pages above the fold.
4. **Image SEO opportunity is real for #15.** Trichome stage charts and harvest readiness diagrams get embedded by other cannabis sites — every embed is a backlink. Watermark them subtly with the domain.

## Things this list deliberately does NOT include

- Broad head terms (`grow cannabis`, `how to grow weed`, `cannabis seeds`) — these are owned by Leafly/Royal Queen Seeds/Sensi Seeds with 10+ year domain ages and millions of backlinks. You can't out-rank them, and chasing them eats budget that long-tail conversions would otherwise capture.
- Strain-specific queries — your site isn't a strain database. Don't compete with Leafly's strain pages.
- "Buy weed online" / delivery / dispensary keywords — wrong intent for this brand. You're education + affiliate, not a retailer.
